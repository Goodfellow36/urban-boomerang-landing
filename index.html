How it works

Three steps.
Infinite impact.

Urban Boomerang runs silently in the background. You shop. The community wins.

01
💳
Link your card

Connect your existing debit or credit card through Plaid's bank-level secure integration. Read-only access. We never touch your money.

02
🛒
Shop as normal

Purchase anything at a participating retailer anywhere in the country — food, gas, beauty, groceries. Your card works exactly as it always has. No apps to open at checkout.

03
🔄
Watch it boomerang

A percentage of every qualifying purchase automatically routes to the community initiative you chose — youth programs, housing, small business funds, athlete foundations.

The bigger picture

Black America's buying power will exceed $2.1 trillion in 2026. Imagine even 1% coming back to the community — automatically.
