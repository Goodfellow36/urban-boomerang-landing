4. How Boomerangs Work

4.1 Qualifying Purchases

A "Boomerang" is triggered when you make a purchase at a participating retailer using your linked payment card. Not all purchases qualify — only purchases at retailers who have enrolled in the Urban Boomerang partner program.

4.2 Boomerang Calculation

The boomerang amount is calculated as:

Boomerang Amount = Purchase Amount × Retailer's Contribution Rate
Platform Fee = Boomerang Amount × 2%
Net to Initiative = Boomerang Amount − Platform Fee

Example: $38.00 purchase at a Gold Partner (3% rate) = $1.14 boomerang. Platform fee: $0.02. Net to initiative: $1.12.

Contribution rates vary by retailer tier: Bronze (1%), Silver (2%), Gold (3%), Platinum (4%+). Rates are set by the retailer and displayed in the app at the time of purchase.

4.3 Hold Period

All boomerangs are held for 30 days from the transaction date to allow for returns, refunds, and disputes. During this period, the boomerang is in "calculated" status and has not yet been disbursed to the initiative.

4.4 Reversals

If Plaid reports that a qualifying transaction was reversed, refunded, or cancelled within the 30-day hold period, the associated boomerang will be automatically reversed. No funds will be disbursed to the initiative for reversed transactions.

4.5 Transaction Matching

We use automated systems to match your transactions to participating retailers. Matching is based on merchant name, location, and other transaction metadata. Match accuracy is not guaranteed. If you believe a transaction was incorrectly matched or missed, you may file a dispute through the app.

5. Community Initiatives and Fund Routing

5.1 Initiative Selection

You may select one active Initiative to receive your boomerangs at any time. You may change your selected Initiative at any time through the app. Changes take effect immediately for future transactions — past boomerangs already directed to an Initiative cannot be redirected.

5.2 Initiative Verification

Urban Boomerang reviews and verifies all Initiatives before listing them on the platform. Verification includes confirming legal entity status, reviewing organizational documents, and verifying banking information. However, Urban Boomerang does not guarantee the activities, programs, or outcomes of any Initiative.

5.3 Disbursements

Funds are disbursed to Initiatives on a monthly basis, typically on the first business day of each month, for all boomerangs that have completed the 30-day hold period. Disbursements are processed via Stripe Connect to the Initiative's verified bank account.

5.4 Minimum Disbursement

Disbursements are made when an Initiative's accumulated balance reaches a minimum of $500. Balances below $500 roll over to the following month.

5.5 Initiative Removal

If an Initiative is removed from the platform (due to closure, non-compliance, or other reasons), undisbursed funds allocated to that Initiative will be held for 90 days. If no replacement designation is made, funds will be redistributed proportionally to remaining active Initiatives, or as directed by applicable law.

6. Platform Fees

Urban Boomerang charges a 2% platform fee on all boomerang amounts. This fee is deducted from the boomerang amount before disbursement to the Initiative. The platform fee covers transaction processing costs, fraud detection, compliance operations, and platform maintenance.

There are no membership fees, subscription fees, or fees for linking your card. The platform fee is the only fee charged to Members.
